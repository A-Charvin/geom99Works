Welcome to our interactive website about the Canadian provinces!

Our website is designed to help kids learn about the provinces and territories of Canada in a fun and engaging way. By using a combination of Bootstrap and ArcGIS Webmap, we've created an interactive map that allows you to explore the different regions of Canada and learn more about their unique characteristics, cultures, and histories. Click on a province to discover interesting facts, such as its capital city, population, and famous landmarks.

We hope you enjoy using our website and learning more about this wonderful country!
